&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Andy Bui is a programmer and 3D artist based in Austin, Texas. He is currently a Software Engineer building web applications at Systems Intelligence. Prior to this, he was at Salesforce, where he began his career on _Hyperforce_, the infrastructure platform.&nbsp;

&nbsp;&nbsp;  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Born in 1996 in Grand Rapids, Michigan, Andy attended Michigan public schools. His early interests included violin, video editing and cinematography, Maplestory animations, cars, and _High School Musical_.  One of his earliest memories was that of enamourment, observing a first-generation Honda CR-V and its full-pillar, vertical tail lights and rear-mounted spare tire. He would later recognize this as the beginning of a sensibility.
